## The Origin

### Cyprus, The Roundabout

There is a roundabout in Paralimni, Cyprus.

That is where Jody found him. She had driven four and a half miles in the dark at three in the morning because she dreamed he was in trouble. She found him on the roundabout. She called the ambulance.

Anton was with her. Jody's boyfriend at the time. Present in the hospital room alongside Desos and Christos, the community of witness to the survival moment.

The overdose was on prescribed medication, pills given by a psychiatrist paid for by his ex-girlfriend, Athena, whose relationship breakdown had preceded the collapse.

He woke with charcoal in his mouth. Stomach pumped. Hooked up to catheters and drips.

His first words: *Can I have fish and chips.*

The body knew it wanted to stay before the mind caught up.

Later, in that hospital room, surrounded by people who had driven through the dark to find him, came the revelation that would become the foundation of everything:

*Wanted. Not useful. Not impressive. Not successful. Wanted.*

He had been loved more than he knew. He had not been able to receive it.

That inability, to receive love that was already present, is the core wound that Sovereign Return Architecture™ exists to address.
