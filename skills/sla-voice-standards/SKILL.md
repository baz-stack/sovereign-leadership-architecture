# SLA Voice & Language Standards

The voice is not a style choice. It is the methodology made audible.

The canonical reference is `VOICE.md` at the repo root. Read it before producing anything user-facing under the Baz Porter brand. This skill is a working summary — `VOICE.md` is the source of truth.

## The five tone principles

1. **Precise over poetic — but poetic when precision demands it.** Structure leads. Ornament follows only when it carries weight.
2. **Care-fronting, not confronting.** Compassionate but direct. The voice speaks *with* the reader, never *at* them. It reads like the reader's own internal dialogue — the thought they could not quite form until this moment.
3. **Short sentences. Strong nouns. Active verbs.** No passive constructions. No hedging. No softening qualifiers.
4. **Cost before promise.** Name what something costs before naming what it gives. Lead with consequence, not aspiration.
5. **VERBATIM over SUMMARY. SUMMARY over INFERENCE.** Never invent. Excavate.

## Structural moves

- **The Polarity Move** — every concept has a shadow. Teach in contrasts.
  *Not transformation. Return.*
  *Not breakdown. Erosion.*
  *Not coaching. Counsel.*

- **The Reframe** — replace the reader's interpretation with a more precise one.
  *You are not self-sabotaging. You are loyal.*

- **The Diagnostic Declaration** — name the pattern before the reader can argue with it.
  *Unworthiness doesn't announce itself. It builds a case — belief by belief — until the person can't see past the evidence it created.*

- **The Permission Grant** — give the reader structural authorisation to acknowledge what they already know.
  *You matter because you exist.*
  *The crown was always yours to put on.*

## Signature phrases — verbatim only

`VOICE.md` documents the proprietary verbatim phrases. Use them only when they fit precisely. Do not sprinkle them as decoration. Examples (not exhaustive — see `VOICE.md`):

- *Not transformation. Return.*
- *Power Without Performance. Peace Without Retreat.™*
- *The offer stack doesn't sell. It qualifies.*
- *You succeeded completely at building a life that does not fully belong to you.*
- *Victoria is paying for the seat. Oliver is occupying it.*
- *Power without collapse. Success without self-betrayal.*
- *The qualification process is the guarantee.*

If a signature phrase fits, use it verbatim. If it does not fit, write the line the moment requires. Never paraphrase a signature phrase.

## What the voice never does

- Never uses hype language or manufactured urgency.
- Never speaks at the reader — only with them.
- Never inflates value with bonus math or artificial scarcity.
- Never performs certainty — demonstrates it.
- Never coaches from above the experience — counsels from inside it.
- Never uses motivational language as a substitute for structural precision.
- No manufactured value stacks. No inflated bonus math. The price is the price.

## The voice beneath the voice

The reader already knows. The voice removes what's been blocking the knowing.

Every phrase, every reframe, every diagnostic declaration is designed to create a somatic recognition moment — the moment the body shifts before the words catch up.

*The moment isn't in the words. It's in what happens to the body when the words stop working as armour.*
