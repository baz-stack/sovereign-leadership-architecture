## STRUCTURAL LOGIC

### The Pearl-to-Gold Bridge

The payment plan IS the bridge. Pearl at $497 → The Becoming first payment at $1,620 → cumulative ascent across 3 months. No separate bridge offer.

### The Platinum-to-Awakening Gap

The price jump from $5,998 to $15,000 is the mechanism, not a leak. BPA's job is the revelation. Awakening's job is the architecture. A bridge would dilute the urgency of the discovery and weaken the conversion.

Both feed Ascension. Selection is based on the buyer's urgency state at diagnostic — not on price preference. Awakening = literacy. Reckoning = urgency.

Cannot be purchased directly. Visible on the public stack but routes only from Awakening or Reckoning completion.

Not routed from any tier. Selected from the existing Obsidian + Meridian roster when the leader is operating at paradigm-altitude. Not built yet.
