## The Conversion Architecture

### The Private Door
The Becoming™ at $4,620 is the first private, one-to-one container in the stack. It does not bridge to Business Profit Accelerator — it qualifies the leader for the full Coming Home™ container. The Silent Collapse Diagnostic™ is the gate: fit is confirmed in 60 minutes before any invitation is extended, so the container only opens to leaders identified inside Silent Collapse™ at the structural level.

### The Operational Pathway
Business Profit Accelerator ($5,998) serves leaders whose constraint is operational scale rather than identity. It is routed from Revenue Architecture System ($497) and from standalone diagnostic conversations, with the $997 8-week course as downsell. This pathway runs parallel to the private counsel track — identity-first leaders enter through The Becoming™; systems-first leaders enter through BPA.

### The Financial Partner Pathway
Genuine constraint and worth distortion require completely different responses. For candidates who meet the architectural standard but face genuine financial constraint, a financing pathway exists. The audit distinguishes between the two. The conversation determines the pathway.

*Everyone will find the finance. The question is whether they've decided they're worth finding it for.*
