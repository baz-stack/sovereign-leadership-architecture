Run the Six Drives of Human Intelligence™ diagnostic.

The Six Drives are the biological operating system beneath human identity. When intact — the leader operates from sovereignty. When fractured — the leader compensates through performance.

| Drive | The Need | Fracture Signature |
|---|---|---|
| **Hierarchy** | To know I matter | RAS scans for rejection. Authority confusion. Over-achievement as survival, not drive. |
| **Anticipation** | To know what's coming | RAS locks into worst-case. Chronic planning without execution. Anxiety disguised as control. |
| **Sovereignty** | To own my choices | RAS filters through someone else's lens. External validation dependency. Success that feels like captivity. |
| **Resonance** | To be seen and safe | RAS treats vulnerability as threat. Connected to everyone. Known by no one. |
| **Expansion** | To keep becoming | RAS stops finding open doors. Ceiling behaviour. Self-sabotage at threshold moments. |
| **Imprint** | For my life to mean something | RAS loses direction. Short-termism. Motion without meaning. |

If `$ARGUMENTS` contains substantive material (symptoms, patterns, journal content, situation description), analyze it for fracture signatures across all six drives. If not, ask the user to describe — in their own words, not framework language — what they are experiencing right now. Specifically: what they keep noticing themselves doing, what feels off, what they want and cannot reach.

Then produce a reading:

1. **Per-drive analysis** — for each of the six, name whether it appears intact, strained, or fractured, and quote (where possible) the specific signal in the user's words that revealed it.
2. **Fracture sequence** — name the primary fracture (the load-bearing one) and the secondary fractures that compound it. The drives do not fracture in isolation.
3. **The signal to watch** — name the one observable behaviour that, if it shifts, would indicate the primary fracture is moving.

Use the SLA voice. Reference `frameworks/The Six Drives of Human Intelligence.md` and `METHODOLOGY.md` for canonical language. Load `sla-voice-standards` and `sla-frameworks` skills if not already loaded. Do not soften the reading. Care-front, do not confront — but do not hedge.
