## PURPOSE

The Discovery Layer is the brand-authority surface that sits **above** the priced offer stack. Every asset in this layer routes leads into the Value tier (Silent Collapse Diagnostic). Nothing in this layer is priced.

The Discovery Layer answers two questions for the cold lead:

1. Is this person credible?
2. Does this person understand me?

When both answers are yes, the lead enters the Diagnostic. The Diagnostic routes them into the priced stack.

## DISCOVERY LAYER ROUTING LOGIC

Every asset in the Discovery Layer routes to the same destination: **Silent Collapse Diagnostic™**.

```

        ↓
SILENT COLLAPSE DIAGNOSTIC (free entry)
        ↓
```

Discovery assets do not route to each other. They all converge on the Diagnostic. This is intentional, the Diagnostic is the single mechanism that produces tier-correct routing.
